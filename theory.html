What is a Turing Machine?

A Turing Machine is a mathematical model of computation introduced by Alan Turing.

Components

  • Tape
  • Head
  • States
  • Transition rules

Working

The machine reads, writes, changes state, and moves left or right.

Applications

  • Computer science theory
  • Algorithm design
  • AI basics
